Why to create?

    India needs to shift from basic farming to more efficient, sustainable, and productive farming.
    This will result in -

  • Improved productivity and efficiency
  • The input prices will be less
  • Focus on quality or value, instead of quantity
  • Organic farming leads to a better quality of food

Features

  • It is an open website for users. Anyone can get information along with the proper links of materials to invest in farming.
  • Flexible for every languages, multi-language feature is provides.
  • It will always have a weather forecasting button on every page for updates of weather according to the Google location (GPS).
  • It has a rating button to keep a check of rates of vegetables in every area.
  • It has the link to various modern farming tools, pesticides, fertilizers and seeds with their specification to buy online.
  • Flexible and responsive website for androids and smart phones.
  • User friendly website and easy to directed.
